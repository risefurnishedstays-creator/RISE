🌳 Sightseeing · 7

Get out and explore the city

Barton Springs Pool

Pools are fed from underground springs and stay around 68–70°F — perfect for swimming.

Covert Park at Mount Bonnell

Take a short hike up Mount Bonnell and enjoy the breathtaking views. You can eat at one of the picnic tables — time your visit to catch the sunset or sunrise!

Lady Bird Lake Trail

Stroll or bike along the Ann and Roy Butler Hike-and-Bike Trail and Boardwalk and take in the beautiful surroundings. You can also stand-up paddleboard, kayak, or canoe in the lake! Street parking is available along South Lakeshore Boulevard between Pleasant Valley Road and Riverside; if you're hiking the trail, you can also park at Festival Beach or other nearby lots.

McKinney Falls State Park

You can hike, swim, fish, and bike in this beautiful state park.

Ann W. Richards Congress Avenue Bat Bridge

Popular destination for bat-watching from March to September. If you visit, be sure to bring a hat in case of bat droppings!

Texas State Capitol

Take a free tour of the Texas State Capitol to learn about the history of Texas and admire the architectural design of the capitol.

Zilker Metropolitan Park

Visit the botanical gardens, Austin Nature and Science Center, and disc golf course in Zilker Metropolitan Park, among others.
